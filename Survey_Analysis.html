      About this section

      When I majored in sociology in high school, I realized how much I love analyzing social trends and understanding their meaning, and surveys are one of the most effective ways to understand public opinion on diverse issues. Over the years, I encountered many situations where a good survey is the one that reflected the situation properly, and I learned statistical and design techniques that would help me analyze the findings.

      Python Exam's Survey

      During my role as the student representative on behalf of the student union, many contacted me claiming that the Python basics test was harder than expected. In order to test this claim, I published a survey for all the students who took the test and examined what had the most distinct effect on their feeling of failure in the test: the lecturer, the degree of attendance in classes or repeated viewing of the course recordings.
