    Market size analysis based on real data

    Includes a summary presentation

    • Download The Project

    In this project I took a fictitious case story about a fashion company that wants to open its first store in one of Tel Aviv's neighborhoods. The data are completely real and are from the municipality's website in cooperation with the Central Bureau of Statistics. Based on this data, I performed an analysis and came to the conclusion in which neighborhood is the most profitable for the fashion company to open a new store.
